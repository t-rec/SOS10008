# Privacy Policy for SOS 10008

**Last updated:** November 30, 2025

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application SOS 10008 ("Application") for mobile devices that was created by Local 10008.

## What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

### User Provided Information
The Application does not require any user registration. The Application allows you to create and save meeting notes locally on your device. These notes are stored securely on your device's internal storage using AsyncStorage and are not transmitted to any external server.

### Automatically Collected Information
The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

## Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

## Do third parties see and/or have access to information obtained by the Application?

No. We do not share your information with third parties. All data created within the app (meeting notes) remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., by sending an email via the Contact feature).

## Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

The Application stores your meeting notes and preferences (language, theme) locally on your device. You can delete your notes at any time within the Application. Uninstalling the Application will remove all locally stored data.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at 10008@ute-sei.org.
